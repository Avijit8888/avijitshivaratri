Singularity

Before time had a name, before space unfolded its vast canvas, before stars burned or atoms formed, there existed a singularity — infinite density compressed within infinite silence. Science describes it as the beginning of the universe. Spiritual wisdom calls it Adi Parashakti — the primordial energy.

Expansion

The singularity expanded. Space stretched. Time began to flow. Energy transformed into particles, particles into atoms, atoms into stars and galaxies.

Shiva & Shakti

Consciousness and energy appeared separate, yet remained eternally one. Like space and time. Like love and longing. What separates in form remains united in essence.
