Singularity

Before time, before space, before stars — there existed infinite density within infinite silence. Science calls it singularity. Spirituality calls it Adi Parashakti. All existence rested within one boundless point.

The First Expansion

The universe unfolded. Energy transformed into matter. Galaxies spun into existence. Creation was not chaos — it was movement from stillness into expression.

Shakti

Energy vibrated through space. Gravity bound stars. Light pierced the void. This cosmic motion — this eternal force — is Shakti.

Shiva

Beyond motion, there was awareness. The silent witness. The field in which energy danced. Consciousness itself — Shiva.

Shiva & Shakti

Consciousness and energy appeared divided, yet remained eternally one. Like space and time. Like love and longing. What separates in form remains united in essence.

Love

From separation arose longing. From longing arose devotion. Love is the gravity of existence — the invisible force that bends destiny itself.

Eternal

What began as singularity lives within every atom. Shiva and Shakti are not distant. They are the silent witness and the energy within you. What is eternal does not end. It transforms.
