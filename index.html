Chapter I

Singularity

Before time had a name, before space unfolded, there existed infinite density within infinite silence. Science calls it singularity. Spirituality calls it Adi Parashakti. All existence rested within one boundless point.

Chapter II

The First Expansion

Space stretched. Time began to flow. Energy transformed into matter. Galaxies emerged from cosmic dust. Creation was not chaos — it was movement from stillness into expression.

Chapter III

Shakti

Energy vibrated through the universe. Stars ignited. Gravity bound existence together. This cosmic motion — this eternal force — is Shakti.

Chapter IV

Shiva

Beyond motion lies awareness — the silent witness. The field in which energy dances. Consciousness itself. Without Shiva, Shakti has no witness.

Chapter V

The Sacred Union

Consciousness and energy appeared separate, yet remained eternally one. Like space and time. Like love and longing. What separates in form remains united in essence.

Chapter VI

Love

From separation arose longing. From longing arose devotion. Love is the gravity of existence — the invisible force bending destiny.

Chapter VII

Eternal

What began as singularity lives within every atom. Within every heartbeat. Within every act of love. What is eternal does not end. It transforms. It returns.
