In the Beginning

There was Singularity.

Singularity

Before time, before space, before atoms or stars, there was infinite density within infinite silence. Science calls it singularity. Spirituality calls it Adi Parashakti. All existence rested within one point of potential.

Expansion

The universe expanded. Energy became matter. Matter formed galaxies. Creation was not chaos — it was movement from stillness into expression.

Shiva & Shakti

Consciousness and energy appeared separate, yet remained eternally one. Shiva — the silent witness. Shakti — the cosmic motion. Love is the gravity that binds them.
